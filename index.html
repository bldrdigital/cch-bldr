About Dr. Campbell

A Healer Who Has
Taught Other Healers

Dr. Timothy R. Campbell graduated from Cleveland Chiropractic College — and then returned to teach there for three and a half years, training the next generation of practitioners before entering private practice in Sherman Oaks.

For over four decades, he has practiced a uniquely whole-body approach: treating not just the spine, but the nutritional, emotional, and energetic systems that govern every patient's health. Patients come from across the Valley — and keep coming back for years — because it works.

What We Treat

Care for the Whole Person

01

Chiropractic Adjustments

Precise, gentle spinal and extremity adjustments that restore alignment and relieve the nerve pressure at the root of pain and limited mobility.

02

Applied Kinesiology

Muscle-testing diagnostics that identify structural, nutritional, and emotional imbalances — revealing the underlying cause rather than just the symptom.

03

Neuro-Emotional Technique

A gentle method for identifying and releasing emotional patterns stored in the body that contribute to chronic pain, fatigue, and stress responses.

04

Nutritional Support

Blood chemistry review and dietary guidance addressing the biochemical foundations of your symptoms — not just their surface expression.

05

Cranial-Sacral Therapy

Light-touch therapy addressing restrictions in the craniosacral system — effective for headaches, jaw tension, neck pain, and nervous system dysregulation.

06

Reiki & Energy Work

Non-invasive energetic support that complements structural and nutritional care — promoting deep relaxation and the body's natural healing response.
